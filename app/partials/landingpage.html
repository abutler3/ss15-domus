Welcome to Domus

A simple meeting notes tracker


Meetings are a fact of life

The portion of the meeting that applies to you is likely small

But it's something you have to remember.

How?

An easy four-step process

  • - Register (if already registered, then log in)
  • - Use the form to enter the meeting name and any details you need to remember
  • - When you need to remember what happened at a specific meeting, just hover over that meeting to see your notes
  • - Wow people with you recall of what happened at meetings.
